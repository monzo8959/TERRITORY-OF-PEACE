ABOUT OUR ACTIVITIES

Peace comes only with victory. That is why our main task is to help our captors and the civilian population to organize a reliable force, especially in the most hotspots. Support of the civilian population is material, psychological and legal. Now all the time we are collecting money for military ammunition and special equipment, experience and forces are given for the purchase, delivery and distribution of food, clothing, hygienic goods, and everything you need.

We provide food for people who are in hot spots, civilians and military servicemen

We provide liquids for hospitals, shelter buildings and military hospitals

We provide our servicemen with uniforms, body armor and other ammunition

we're talking about one

We are ordinary people who from the very beginning of the war radically changed their lives and began to help those who needed it. All of our volunteers from different parts of the country... But we were united by one thing - the desire to live in a peaceful country! What to do - Ukraine is one!
